How can you help in the fight to end hunger?

To put an end to hunger, we need everyone to work together to get the job done. It's a fight we must all take part in to win and here at the Food For Thought organization, we need you! Below we have listed just a couple of ways you can do your part to end hunger in your community.

Ways to help in the fight to end hunger during the covid-19 outbreak

Volunteer at your local food banks and charity events

Pack and sort food for the community and those who it need

Deliver meals to homes who need food to eat while they try staying safe indoors

Assisting mobile pantries and drive-thru pantries

Trust me, they will be greatful you did!
